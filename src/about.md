# About

I'm Talip Göksu, a software engineer and team lead based in Germany.

I work across mobile, web, and backend systems — from native apps to modern TypeScript stacks and the tooling around them. My day-to-day work is split between leading engineering teams, shaping product architecture, and building software that helps other builders move faster with more confidence.

I care about thoughtful systems: clear architecture, useful tests, calm processes, and code that earns its complexity. I like software that is practical, durable, and honest about its trade-offs.

This blog is where I think out loud about engineering, leadership, product ideas, AI-assisted development, discipline, and the small decisions that shape good work.

Expect notes with intention rather than hot takes: what I am learning, what I am building, and what seems worth remembering.
